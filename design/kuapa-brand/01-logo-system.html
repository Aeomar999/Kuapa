KUAPA · BRAND

Logo system

Kuapa — from Twi kua (farming) + pa (good): good farming. The mark grows the K from a stem and two leaves, with a gold seed at the node — produce moving from farm to market. Bold, flat and legible at 24 px, in the same confident app-icon language as the original Bexiemart mark.

Why it works

K is the crop

The stem and two leaves literally spell K — the brand initial is a growing plant, so the mark says “good farming” without a single word.

The gold seed

One harvest-gold seed sits at the node where growth starts — the “pa” (good) in Kuapa, and a nod to Momo gold in the wallet.

Built for the app

Flat, single-weight shapes survive 24 px favicons, USSD posters and low-end screens. Same rounded-square language as the marketplace icon set.
